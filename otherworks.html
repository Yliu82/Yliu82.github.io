Other Works-12x12x12 seat

what makes a seat? Design a structure to support a person in a way that is interesting and relevant given its location. Consider your seat’s design, look and relationship to the site.

Thinking Process: Ideation
Exploring early conceptual directions through quick sketches and structural play.
Modular Possibilities
A flexible configuration system showcasing how the modular seating adapts to diverse environments, offering dynamic arrangements for personal, communal, and transitional spaces.
Assembly Instructions
A step-by-step visual guide designed to ensure intuitive, efficient, and tool-assisted assembly for users of all skill levels, promoting clarity and confidence throughout the process.
Force Analysis
Analyzing load distribution and stability through basic engineering evaluation.
Material Selection
Comparing potential materials for comfort, weight, and environmental impact.
Project Poster
Concise one-page summary highlighting the problem, process, and solution.
Final Rendering
Photorealistic renderings simulating the design in real-life contexts.
